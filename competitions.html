Rules

Game settings

  • Room name: bonk.tk - 1vs1 / 2vs2
  • Password: Recommended
  • Max players: 3
  • Time limit: None
  • Score limit: 2x 3 goals (change sides at halftime)

Referee

You need a referee for every match you play. After the match, referee is obligated to report the result as fast as possible.

Host

Before you start the game, you should do a little lag test and refresh or change the host, if it lags. The referee makes sure it's lag free and tries to solve lag issues.

Opponent quits the match

If your opponent quits the match and doesn't respond within 10 minutes, you get as many goals as needed to finish the match.

Behavior

In order to make the bonk.tk enjoyable for everyone, you should be nice to each other! Joking around sometimes is okay, but you should know the limits of this. If somebody insults you, you can report it to one of the admins and explain the situation.
